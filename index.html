Why story telling mindfullness?

The dynamic, inventive, and participatory nature of storytelling allows it to connect with a portion of our minds and bodies that makes us feel entire. It broadens our perspective of who we are and the abilities we already possess. It also strengthens our conviction that, at our heart, we are imaginative, playful beings, and that it is this aspect of ourselves that enables us to change, develop, and heal.

Club Offers

A Mindful Storytelling Club can offer a unique and enriching experience for its members by combining the art of storytelling with mindfulness practices.

Mindful Storytelling Workshops

Members can participate in regular workshops to learn and practice mindfulness techniques such as meditation, deep breathing, and body scan exercises. They can also focus on the art of storytelling, including story structure, character development, and effective communication.

Self-Discovery and Reflection

Mindful story telling encourages self-exploration and self-expression. Members can discover more about themselves as they share personal stories. Reflecting on one's life experiences through storytelling can be therapeutic and contribute to personal growth.

Building a Supportive Community

The club can provide a welcoming and inclusive community where members can connect, share, and support each other. Creating a sense of belonging and mutual understanding through storytelling and mindfulness can be incredibly powerful.
